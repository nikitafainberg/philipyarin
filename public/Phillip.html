ABOUT

Philipp Yarin was born in 1988 in Moscow. He is a graduate of The All-Russian state institute of cinematography named after S. A. Gerasimov(VGIK) After graduation he worked at animation studios "Animos", "Pilot", "Pchela", "Master-Film" as a director and art-director. He has awards at international animation festivals. Philipp is engaged in painting, graphics and sculpture. Also there is experience in book illustration.


Recent Exhibitions:
2011 - Group exhibition "Course number four". Gallery Solyanka VPA. Moscow, Russia
2011 - “We and the forms”. Bogoliubov Arts Library. Moscow, Russia
2011 - “2016 - Museum of Silver age, Moscow — exhibition designer and prop maker
2017 - Special project “The Writer's Kitchen”. Museum-Reserve of A.P. Chekhov “Melikhovo”.Russia — exhibition designer, illustrator, prop maker
